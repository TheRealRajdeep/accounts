# Accounts SDK

Build account experiences on Tempo without locking your app to one signing model. The Accounts SDK routes app requests through adapters, so the same product flow can run through Tempo Wallet, domain-bound WebAuthn, or your own signing infrastructure.

## Choose an adapter

| Adapter | Use it when |
| --- | --- |
| [Tempo Wallet](/docs/adapters/tempo-wallet) | You want a universal wallet without owning auth or signing infrastructure. |
| [WebAuthn](/docs/adapters/webauthn) | You want domain-bound passkey accounts on your own app origin. |
| [Custom adapter](/docs/adapters/custom) | You want to connect Privy, AWS KMS, Turnkey, internal signers, or your own hosted wallet. |
